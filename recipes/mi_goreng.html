Mi Goreng

Description

Mie Goreng (or Mee Goreng) is an Indonesian noodle dish thatâ€™s also found in Malaysia and other parts of South East Asia. With a sticky, savoury sweet sauce, noodles are tossed with chicken, prawns, vegetables and signature egg ribbons. A street food favourite!.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Indomie Mi Goreng, use 2 packets if you're very hungry
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 small sprig green onion, to garnish

Steps

  1. Boil 1l of water
  2. Put noodles into the boiling water.
  3. While the noodles are boiling, add the seasoning in the little packets to a bowl.
  4. When noodles are al dente, transfer to bowl and add raw eggs and garnish.
